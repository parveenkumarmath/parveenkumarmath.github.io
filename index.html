About

I am a PhD Research Scholar in the Department of Mathematics at Indian Institute of Technology Ropar under the supervision of Dr. Manmohan Vashisth. My research interests include inverse problems for partial differential equations, with emphasis on uniqueness and stability results using Carleman estimates.

Research Interests

Inverse Problems

Coefficient identification and reconstruction from boundary measurements for PDEs.

Partial Differential Equations

Heat equations, wave equations, Schrödinger equations and parabolic systems.

Carleman Estimates

Uniqueness and stability analysis in inverse problems.
