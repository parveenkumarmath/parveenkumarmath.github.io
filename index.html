About

I am a PhD Research Scholar in the Department of Mathematics at IIT Ropar, working under the supervision of Dr. Manmohan Vashisth. My research lies in inverse problems for partial differential equations, including heat equations, wave equations, Carleman estimates, and coefficient reconstruction.

Research Interests

Inverse Problems

Recovery of coefficients from partial boundary measurements.

Partial Differential Equations

Heat equations, wave equations, Schrödinger equations and related systems.

Carleman Estimates

Uniqueness and stability results using weighted estimates.
